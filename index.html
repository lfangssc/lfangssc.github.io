Modern AB Testing using Probabilistic Programming

AB testing is the most commonly used technique in marketing. The traditional statistics method is hard to interpret for an audience who doesn't have a statistics background. The p-value and accepting or rejecting a null hypothesis are not straightforward to understand.

We use probabilistic programming to simulate the conversion rates and visualize the sampling distributions of controlled and treatment groups. The simulation approach makes AB testing remarkably easy to understand.
